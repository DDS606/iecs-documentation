# Stakeholder Analysis

The IECS project stakeholders are analyzed based on their level of power and interest in the project.

## Stakeholder Matrix

| Stakeholder | Power | Interest | Management Strategy |
|-------------|-------|----------|---------------------|
| Project Sponsor | High | High | Manage closely |
| Project Manager | High | High | Manage closely |
| Development Team | Medium | High | Keep engaged |
| Energy Consumers | Medium | High | Keep informed |
| Utility Companies | High | Medium | Keep satisfied |
| Environmental Organizations | Low | Medium | Monitor |

The stakeholder matrix helps prioritize communication and engagement strategies during the project lifecycle.
Stakeholders with high power and high interest require close management, while stakeholders with lower power can be monitored periodically.
